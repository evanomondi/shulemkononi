About Shule Mkononi

Shule Mkononi is a complete management system for schools. It Manages Administrative control along with standard access to teacher, student and parent for all necessary facilities.

Cutting to the heart of student achievement, Shule Mkononi is re-imagining the future of Student Management Systems for principals, boards, parents, teachers and students.

  • Exam marks managementManage exam marks of all students in the school. With:
    • • Question Papers
    • • Customisable grading system
    • • Intuitive marks management
    • • Tabulation sheet on marks
    • Send Exam marks by text
  • Class ManagemntThe ClassRoom Manager is a powerful, flexible tool to facilitate recording, reporting and analysing of student-centric learning data. Optimised to ensure efficiency for both teachers and administrators, ClassRoom Manager can alleviate teacher workload and meet the individual needs of schools.
  • Library ManagementManage the school library easily by organising books by class, issue books from school library, keep track of issued books and set a librarian to manage the school library.
  • Student ManagementShule mkononi Student Details meets the needs of any school, with essential tools for the school office to manage all administrative tasks relating to students. Because Shule Mkononi is manage in the cloud, student details and key information can be securely shared and accessed at any time.
  • Fee ManagementShool fees can be managed at a single place with help in, invoice generation for students, student specific payment history, expense management, multiple payment on same invoice and many more.
  • Web and Mobile AppEverythng relating to the school, management and students is a accessible under one customisable we and mobile app
